Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

MedIntel AI is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Symptom Input: The symptoms you enter are processed in real-time for analysis. We do not permanently store your symptom descriptions on our servers.
  • Local Storage: Your search history and saved analyses are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service, including feature usage and error logs. This data cannot be used to identify you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide health intelligence analysis based on your symptoms
  • To maintain your local search history and bookmarks
  • To improve our AI algorithms and knowledge base
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

Your privacy is our priority:

  • All symptom analysis is performed in real-time and not stored on our servers
  • Your history and bookmarks are stored only in your browser's local storage
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect any data transmitted
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties

4. Cookies and Local Storage

MedIntel AI uses:

  • Local Storage: To save your theme preference, search history, and bookmarks locally on your device
  • Session Cookies: For technical functionality (if applicable)

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or using the "Clear History" option in the application.

5. Third-Party Services

MedIntel AI does not integrate with third-party services that would have access to your symptom data. We may use standard web fonts from Google Fonts, which are loaded according to Google's privacy policy.

6. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • View your search history and saved analyses at any time
  • Delete individual history items or clear all history
  • Remove saved bookmarks
  • Clear all local data by clearing browser storage

7. Medical Disclaimer

MedIntel AI is for educational purposes only. We do not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals for medical concerns. Never disregard professional medical advice because of information you received from this platform.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us through the appropriate channels.
